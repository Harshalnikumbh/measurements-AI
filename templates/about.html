About Us

Learn more about our AI-powered body measurement technology

Our Mission

We are dedicated to revolutionizing the way people measure their bodies. Using advanced AI and computer vision technology, we provide accurate body measurements from simple photos, making custom fitting accessible to everyone.

How It Works

Our system uses state-of-the-art 3D human mesh recovery (HMR2) technology to analyze your photos. Simply upload front and side view images, enter your height and weight, and our AI will calculate precise measurements for your neck, chest, waist, hips, shoulders, and arms.

Why Choose Us

  • Accurate measurements powered by AI
  • Quick and easy process
  • No special equipment needed
  • Privacy-focused - your images are processed and deleted
  • Perfect for online shopping and custom tailoring

Our Technology

We leverage cutting-edge deep learning models including HMR2 (Human Mesh Recovery) and advanced 3D mesh analysis to extract accurate body measurements from 2D images. Our algorithms are trained on extensive datasets to ensure reliability across different body types.
